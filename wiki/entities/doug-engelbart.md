# Doug Engelbart

## Overview

Computer scientist and inventor who read Bush's "As We May Think" shortly after publication. Keeping the Memex concept in mind, he invented foundational technologies: the mouse, word processor, hyperlink, and new media concepts.

## Key Information

- **Influence**: Inspired by Vannevar Bush's Memex essay
- **Inventions**:
  - Computer mouse
  - Word processor
  - Hyperlink
  - New media concepts
- **Philosophy**: Technologies were "enabling technologies" for the broader vision of augmented knowledge work
